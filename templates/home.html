Navigating the complexities of student life often involves addressing mental health challenges. Our counseling platform offers tailored support to enhance students' well-being and empower them on their academic journey.

Empowering International Students to enhance their well-being and academic journey in Ireland.

Counselors provide vital guidance and strategies to navigate through stressful times with resilience and well-being.

An online platform for student counseling offers accessible support that transcends barriers of time and location.

How It works ?

Register on the portal

As an initial action, both students and counselors can complete their registration on the website.

Admin

In the administrative section, the administrator can review all registered users within the system and grant approval for registration requests.

Counsellors

Students have the ability to browse through available counselors, view their schedules, and reserve appointments with them as per their convenience.

Virtual Counseling

After the counselor is assigned, the consultation can be conducted online, serving as the concluding step.
